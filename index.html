Who We Are

Built at the Intersection of Science & Craft

InkAxis was born from a simple frustration — great researchers struggle with web presence, and great developers rarely understand academic writing. We bridge that gap.

We are a team of AI engineers and technical writers with hands-on experience building real-world projects, publishing research papers, and crafting modern websites. We bring the same dedication and precision to every deliverable.

I bring firsthand international research experience — having worked as a researcher on global collaborative projects alongside teams and institutions across different countries. That background means I understand international publication standards, cross-border academic expectations, and what it actually takes to get work recognized on a world stage.
